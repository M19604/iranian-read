PEACE COUNCIL
Today, November 1st, Iran has been accepted and approved to be in the Peace Council. The official membership will begin on November 4th. This is one step to making Iran a greater, stronger country than it is currently.
MODERATION TEAM
Iran's leader, Luke Skutt, has been accepted into the moderation team for the country game. To have a full membership status, an interview will take place on November 2nd to determine it.
